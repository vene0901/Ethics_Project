About Us

Welcome to our project on the ethical implications of AI in job screening! Our team consists of Hiba, Ikran, and Zuri, passionate individuals committed to exploring the intersection of technology and ethics.

Project Focus

Our project delves into the ethical considerations surrounding the increasing use of AI in job screening processes. We aim to shed light on the potential biases and injustices inherent in these systems, with a particular focus on issues such as AI bias, facial recognition, and data privacy.

Motivation

As women of color in the tech industry, we recognize the importance of ensuring fairness and inclusivity in AI technologies. The prevalence of biased algorithms in job screening poses significant challenges, perpetuating inequalities and hindering opportunities for marginalized groups. Through our project, we seek to raise awareness about these issues and advocate for more equitable practices in AI adoption.

Expertise

Each member of our team brings unique expertise to this project. Hiba specializes in data analysis and machine learning, Ikran has a background in ethics and social justice, and Zuri brings experience in user experience design and human-computer interaction. Together, we are dedicated to conducting thorough research and providing valuable insights into this complex topic.

Join Us

We invite you to explore our website and join us in our mission to promote fairness and transparency in AI-driven job screening. Together, we can work towards a future where technology is a force for positive change.
